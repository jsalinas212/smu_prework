Who is Tosin Abasi?




Bio

Tosin Abasi is a Nigerian-American progressive rock/metal musician and is the lead guitarist for Animals as Leaders. His unique playing style incorporates "thumping" and hybrid picking.

Tosin has played in several other bands, including PSI and Reflux, and has also played along side Steve Vai, Yngwei Malmsteen, and Zakk Wylde.
